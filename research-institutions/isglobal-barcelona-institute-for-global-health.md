## General Description

Since 2022, ISGlobal has delivered services to promote and enable open source practices within its institution. This work is based in Digital Innovation within its TTO. 

## Core Objectives

*Current services include:*

- Open source licensing guidance 
- License compatibility and dependency risk 
- Decision support on open source versus proprietary pathways 
- Dual licensing and relicensing strategy 
- Research contract interpretation for software and open source obligations 
- Advice on IP strategy that includes patents, startups, and open source 
- Advocacy and policy development inside the institution
- Bridging open source with open data, open science, and broader knowledge assets
- Contributor rights and authorship management

Additional areas of engagement currently under development:
- Training and awareness raising on open source practice (especially in connection with digital and regulatory aspects
